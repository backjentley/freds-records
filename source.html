Fred's Sourcing Service

Can't Find It?
We'll Source It.

Just because it's not on our shelf doesn't mean we can't get it. With 50+ years of supplier relationships, exclusive distributor access, and deep connections across the global vinyl network — if it exists on wax, we can track it down.

How It Works
1
Tell Us What You Want
Artist, album, pressing, year — the more detail you give us, the faster we find it. Specific pressing variants, matrix numbers, and country of origin all help narrow the search.
2
We Hunt It Down
Our buyers check distributor catalogues, private collections, estate sales, and trusted dealer networks across North America. If it exists, there's a good chance we know somebody who has it.
3
We Quote & Confirm
Once located, we email you a price (always fair market value), condition report with photos, and estimated shipping time. You approve before we charge anything.
4
Straight to Your Door
Graded, packed in triple-ply cruciform mailers, and shipped direct. Same quality guarantee as everything in our shop.

Common Questions
Is there a fee for searching? expand_more
No. We only charge when we find your record and you approve the purchase. The sourcing service itself is completely free.
How are records priced? expand_more
We price at fair market value based on Discogs data, condition, and rarity. We'll always give you a price with the option to approve or pass before we charge anything.
What if you can't find it? expand_more
If we can't locate the record within 30 days, we'll let you know and add it to our watch list. When one surfaces in the future, you'll get first dibs.
Can I request multiple records? expand_more
Absolutely. Submit as many requests as you like. Each one gets treated individually by our buying team.
Do you ship internationally? expand_more
Yes. We ship worldwide. US orders are tariff-exempt under the "Informational Materials" statute. All records ship in triple-ply cruciform mailers.
