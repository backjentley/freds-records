02

Seven Revenue Engines. One Platform.

This isn't just a website. It's seven independent revenue streams, each backed by deep research and proven e-commerce psychology, all running through a single Shopify-powered storefront.

bolt The Friday Drop

All high-value used arrivals ($50+) drop every Friday at 12:00 PM NST. Countdown timers, teaser images, and accelerated checkout (Shop Pay, Apple Pay) turn passive browsing into a weekly competitive event. Supreme proved the model. We bring it to vinyl.

redeem Mystery Crates

3 curated VG+ records in a sealed box for $40. Genre-themed (90s Grunge, Newfoundland Trad, Jazz Essentials). 1-in-50 chance of pulling a $200+ chase record. Clears slow-moving used inventory at premium margins while generating viral UGC on TikTok/Instagram.

storefront Branded Merch Engine

You sold 300 hoodies at $80 each by hand-delivering them. With an automated Shopify store, print-on-demand for testing and bulk runs for proven winners, this scales to 10,000+ units annually. Global shipping. Zero hand-delivery.

flight_takeoff Tourist Capture Net

Thousands walk through 198 Duckworth every summer and leave forever. Shopify POS e-receipts and dynamic QR codes capture tourist emails at zero friction. Automated "Newfoundland Hangover" Klaviyo flows trigger 3 days later: "Missing St. John's? We ship global."

inventory_2 National "Sell Us Your Wax" Portal

A conditional-logic intake form lets collectors submit their collection details. AI-assisted cataloging cross-references Discogs market data to generate instant offers. Automated reverse-shipping labels and Store Credit payouts. Turn Canada's attics into Fred's inventory.

security Record Store Day Defense

RSD exclusives are worth 5-8x retail. Multi-layer bot mitigation (CAPTCHAs, virtual queues, honeypots) ensures inventory reaches real fans. Plus, the "Informational Materials" tariff exemption (50 USC 1702) drops US cross-border shipping to $8-12/record.

local_shipping New Release Direct-Ship

Customers pre-order new releases through the site. Orders route straight to distributors for direct-to-door shipping. No extra shelf space, no extra staff time. The store doesn't touch the box. Pure margin on every unit.
