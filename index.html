The Legacy · Since 1972

"A Positive Force of Nature"

Fred Brokenshire opened the doors at 198 Duckworth Street in 1972, at the age of 19. What began as a humble record shop became the beating heart of Newfoundland's music scene - launching The Irish Descendants, managing Damhnait Doyle, and pioneering Atlantic distribution through Duckworth Distribution.

"Fred's is to Newfoundland what Amoeba is to LA. It's not just a store - it's an institution. The kind of place where you walk in for one record and walk out three hours later with a bag full of things you didn't know you needed."

- Atlantic Business Magazine, 2025
