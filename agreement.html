Section 01

Purpose & Scope

This Agreement establishes the terms under which Summit Digital Inc. ("Summit") will design, build, launch, and manage an e-commerce operation for Fred's Records Ltd. ("Fred's Records") at fredsrecords.com. The goal of this partnership is to generate a new, incremental online revenue stream for Fred's Records — one that does not interfere with in-store operations, does not require additional workload from existing floor staff, and is consistent with the values and legacy of the store.

All work performed under this Agreement is in addition to Fred's Records' existing in-store business. The in-store operation, existing staff responsibilities, and Tony Plowman's daily workflow remain unchanged and are not subject to this Agreement.

Section 02

Services Provided by Summit

  • Design and development of the Shopify e-commerce storefront at fredsrecords.com
  • Configuration of inventory management middleware (Discogs sync, POS integration, digital quarantine bin system)
  • Integration and setup of email marketing platform (Klaviyo), including automated flows: Welcome Series, New Arrivals, Diaspora Care Package, Wantlist Alerts
  • Shipping carrier integration via aggregator API (Shippo or equivalent) including NL weather delay protocol
  • Photography station setup specification and staff training for product listing workflow
  • SEO configuration including structured data markup (MusicAlbum JSON-LD), Google Merchant feed, and Discogs SEO strategy
  • Ongoing digital marketing management: weekly "Fresh in the Bins" drop execution, Instagram content calendar, and email list growth
  • Monthly reporting: online revenue, conversion rate, email list growth, and top-selling SKUs
  • All reasonable technical maintenance and platform updates during the term of this Agreement
Section 03

Compensation & Revenue Share

How Summit gets paid: Summit receives no retainer, no monthly management fee, and no upfront payment other than the one-time setup contribution in Section 3.2. Summit is compensated exclusively as a percentage of online revenue. If the online store generates no revenue, Summit receives no compensation.
Revenue Share Rate 10% of Gross Online Revenue. "Gross Online Revenue" means the total value of completed online orders placed through fredsrecords.com, before deduction of Shopify processing fees, shipping carrier costs, or packaging expenses. Orders that are subsequently refunded in full are excluded. Partial refunds are deducted proportionally.
What Is Included All product sales completed through the fredsrecords.com Shopify storefront: used vinyl, new releases, merchandise, gift cards, and any future product categories launched under this agreement.
What Is Excluded In-store sales, phone orders, Discogs marketplace sales (unless routed through the Shopify storefront), wholesale orders, and any revenue generated entirely outside the online platform.
Payment Schedule Summit invoices Fred's Records on the 1st of each calendar month for the prior month's online revenue. Fred's Records remits payment within 10 business days of invoice receipt. Payments are made by e-transfer or pre-authorized bank transfer.
Reporting & Verification Summit provides a monthly revenue report with a direct link to the Shopify revenue dashboard. Fred's Records retains read-only Shopify dashboard access at all times and may verify reported figures independently. Disputes must be raised within 15 days of invoice receipt.
One-Time Setup Contribution Fred's Records agrees to a one-time contribution of $________ CAD (to be confirmed between parties) toward the cost of initial platform setup, photography equipment, and third-party software licensing. This amount is payable upon signing. It is not a retainer and does not reduce the ongoing revenue share rate.
Section 04

Term, Trial Period & Exit Clause

Initial Trial Period 90 days from the date the Shopify storefront accepts its first public order ("Launch Date"). During this period, either party may terminate the Agreement without penalty and without cause.
Termination During Trial Either party gives 14 days written notice to terminate. All work completed and assets created up to the termination date remain the property of Fred's Records Ltd. Summit has no claim over the domain, Shopify account, email list, product photography, or any digital asset. Summit transfers all administrative access within 48 hours of termination.
Continuation After Trial If neither party terminates within the 90-day trial, the Agreement automatically converts to a 12-month rolling term, renewable annually by mutual written agreement.
Termination After Trial After the 90-day trial, either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Summit provides a complete handover package to Fred's Records within 14 days of the termination date, including all platform credentials, automation documentation, and a 60-day email/SMS archive.
Performance Review At Day 60 from Launch and at every 90-day interval thereafter, both parties review online revenue performance against the projections outlined in the attached Appendix A. If Month 12 online revenue falls below 50% of projection for two consecutive quarters, either party may invoke an early exit with only 14 days notice.
Section 05

Digital Asset Ownership

The principle: Fred's Records owns everything. Summit operates as a trusted service provider. Upon any termination of this Agreement, Fred's Records retains full, unencumbered ownership of all digital assets with no conditions attached.
Shopify Account The Shopify account is registered to, billed to, and owned by Fred's Records Ltd. Summit holds "Collaborator" access only. Fred's Records holds the Owner role at all times.
Domain fredsrecords.com is owned by Fred's Records Ltd. and remains so regardless of the status of this Agreement. Summit does not manage the domain registrar account.
Customer Email List All email subscribers, SMS opt-ins, and customer data collected through fredsrecords.com are the sole property of Fred's Records Ltd. Summit may not use this data for any purpose other than managing the Fred's Records online store.
Product Photography All photographs taken of Fred's Records inventory under this Agreement are owned by Fred's Records Ltd.
Brand & Intellectual Property The Fred's Records name, logo, store identity, and all associated trademarks remain the exclusive property of Fred's Records Ltd. Summit may reference the partnership in its portfolio with Fred's Records' prior written consent.
Code & Technical Work Custom Shopify theme code, Liquid templates, automation scripts, and third-party app configurations created under this Agreement are assigned to Fred's Records Ltd. upon creation. Summit retains the right to use non-proprietary methods and general approaches in other engagements.
Section 06

Mutual Obligations

Fred's Records Agrees To
  • Provide Summit with accurate inventory data and timely access to records selected for online listing
  • Designate a primary point of contact (Murray Brokenshire) for operational decisions
  • Provide a dedicated physical space ("Digital Quarantine Bin") for records queued for online sale
  • Remit Summit's monthly revenue share invoice within 10 business days of receipt
  • Maintain the Shopify account subscription in good standing (monthly subscription billed to Fred's Records)
  • Provide honest feedback within 5 business days on all submitted marketing content for approval
  • Comply with CASL (Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation) requirements, including record-keeping of all opt-in consents
Summit Agrees To
  • Deliver a functional Shopify storefront accepting public orders within 45 days of signing
  • Provide monthly revenue and performance reports by the 5th of each month
  • Never use Fred's Records customer data for any purpose outside this engagement
  • Obtain Fred's Records' written approval before publishing any marketing content on Fred's behalf
  • Maintain operational access to the Shopify platform during agreed business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm NST)
  • Respond to technical issues affecting the storefront within 4 business hours
  • Provide a complete written handover package within 14 days of any termination
Section 07

Returns, Disputes & Chargebacks

Return Policy (Used Vinyl) Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery if the item is demonstrably misgraded relative to the published grade. "Demonstrably misgraded" means: the item plays with an audible skip not noted in the listing, or the visual grade differs by more than one tier from the published Goldmine grade. Buyer pays return shipping. Fred's Records issues a full refund upon receipt and inspection.
Return Policy (New Vinyl & Merch) Returns accepted within 30 days if the item is unopened or defective. Buyer pays return shipping on unopened items. Fred's Records covers shipping on defective items.
Chargeback Responsibility Chargebacks resulting from Summit's operational errors (incorrect item shipped, wrong price charged) are Summit's responsibility. Chargebacks resulting from customer fraud or buyer's remorse are Fred's Records' responsibility (borne by Shopify Payments as standard).
Revenue Share on Returns If an order is fully refunded, the corresponding Summit revenue share for that order is credited against the next monthly invoice. Partial refunds are deducted proportionally.
Section 08

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the financial terms of this Agreement confidential. Neither party will disclose the revenue share rate, the setup contribution amount, or the monthly revenue figures to any third party without the other party's written consent, except as required by law or for the purposes of legal or financial advisors bound by professional confidentiality obligations.

This clause does not prevent Fred's Records from disclosing the existence of its partnership with Summit to family members, employees, or advisors as needed for the operation of the business.

Section 09

Limitation of Liability

Summit's total cumulative liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total revenue share payments received by Summit in the three months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Summit is not liable for lost profits, indirect damages, or consequential damages arising from platform outages, third-party service failures (Shopify, Klaviyo, Discogs), or shipping carrier delays beyond Summit's reasonable control.

Fred's Records is responsible for ensuring all products listed online comply with applicable Canadian laws, including but not limited to accurate product grading, CASL-compliant email collection, and applicable provincial sales tax registration obligations.

Section 10

Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Newfoundland and Labrador and the applicable federal laws of Canada. Any disputes arising from this Agreement that cannot be resolved through good-faith negotiation between the parties shall be referred to mediation before any legal proceedings are commenced. Both parties agree to a minimum 30-day mediation period before litigation.

Section 11

Entire Agreement & Amendments

This Agreement, together with Appendix A (Revenue Projections) and Appendix B (Scope of Work Timeline), constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior oral or written discussions. Amendments must be agreed in writing and signed by both parties.
